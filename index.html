Select photo

Select the photo you want to convert.

You can also take a picture now by pressing the camera button.

When you have selected a photo, press the right button.

Apply filter

Click the filter in the list at the bottom of the screen to apply it.

Deepsketch uses the deeplearning technology to make realistic drawing effect.

Apply color

To apply the original color to the converted image, click the drop-shaped button on the left of the screen.

Blend between original and filtered

Swap the converted image to the left and right.

You can mix the original image and the converted image at an appropriate ratio.

Save and share

You can save the converted artwork to your phone by clicking the Save button at the top right of the screen.

Share your transformed artwork with your friends by clicking the Share button in the middle right of the screen.
